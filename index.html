Welcome to Trichy

Tiruchirapalli, commonly known as Trichy, is a vibrant city in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It boasts a rich historical heritage, with landmarks like the Rock Fort Temple and Srirangam Temple. Trichy is culturally diverse, celebrating festivals with zeal and offering a range of South Indian cuisine. It's also an economic hub, home to industries like BHEL and educational institutions like NIT Trichy. The city has excellent connectivity through its international airport and road/rail networks, making it a key transportation hub. Trichy's tourism attractions include the Kallanai Dam, Puliyancholai Waterfalls, and St. John's Church, making it a fascinating destination for travelers.
